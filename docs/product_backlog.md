# DeafNav Product Backlog

| ID | User Story | Priority | Story Points |
|----|------------|----------|--------------|
| US01 | As a deaf user, I want to feel a vibration when the train is approaching so I don't miss it. | HIGH | 8 |
| US02 | As a user, I want to see a live transcript of station announcements on my mobile app. | HIGH | 5 |
| US03 | As a user, I want to talk to an interpreter via video/chat in case of an emergency. | MEDIUM | 13 |
| US04 | As a user, I want to monitor my stress levels (pulse) to ensure I am safe in crowded areas. | MEDIUM | 5 |
| US05 | As an administrator, I want to push urgent announcements to all users in the station. | HIGH | 3 |
| US06 | As a user, I want the app to work offline if I lose Wi-Fi in the tunnel. | MEDIUM | 8 |
| US07 | As a user, I want to see sign language video loops for standard safety alerts. | LOW | 5 |

## Sprint 1 Goals (Core MVP)
- US01: Proximity Vibration logic.
- US02: Real-time Transcript Feed.
- US05: Push Announcements.
